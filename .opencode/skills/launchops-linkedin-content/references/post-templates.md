## Template 5: Worried About Results
```
Worried your leads aren't converting?

Here's why:

You're too focused on the output — more leads, better ads, bigger budget.
Not the process that turns a lead into a booked call.

Try this instead:

→ Fix response time before you spend another pound on ads. Speed is the conversion lever nobody talks about.
→ Qualify conversationally — not with a form. Back-and-forth converts at a completely different rate.
→ Follow up until they say no. 30 days. 60 days. 90 days. The money is in the follow-up most businesses never do.

Do this consistently.

→ Study your best booked calls — what did those leads have in common? Build qualification around that pattern.
→ Respond before your competitor does — first to have a real conversation wins most of the time.
→ Reactivate before you buy new — you have cold leads that cost money to generate. Work them first.

No matter the industry, the lesson is the same.

A pipeline that responds fast, follows up consistently, and never lets a lead go cold — will outperform a better offer with a slow process every time.

P.S. Which is your biggest gap — response time, follow-up consistency, or reactivation? Drop it below. 👇
```
