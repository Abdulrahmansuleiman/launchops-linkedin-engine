# Weekly Posting Calendar — LaunchOps LinkedIn

## The 7-Day Structure

| Day | Format | Audience | Content pillar |
|-----|--------|----------|----------------|
| Monday | Storytelling / Pain | Founders | Lead response speed |
| Tuesday | Hard truth / Opinion | Agency owners | Setter dependency / hiring trap |
| Wednesday | Behind the build | All | Pipeline architecture / real work |
| Thursday | Education / Insider tip | Builders + freelancers | Tactical how-to |
| Friday | Belief shift / Skeptic | AI skeptics + founders | Industry gets it wrong |
| Saturday | Education or Personality | All | Value drop or personal brand |
| Sunday | Money angle / Reactivation | Agency + local biz | Cold lead / CRM money |

## Post Time
All posts: 8:00–9:00 AM EST
Never post after 3:00 PM

## Weekly Content Mix
- 2 storytelling/social proof posts
- 2 opinion/hard truth posts
- 1 education post
- 1 behind the build post
- 1 personality post (weekend)

## Monthly Repeating Themes
- Week 1: Founder pain (time, admin, manual work)
- Week 2: System vs. old way (before/after, pipeline proof)
- Week 3: Industry truth (what's wrong, what works, reframe)
- Week 4: Social proof + personality (results, tennis, human moment)

## Impression Targets
- Each post: 1,000+ impressions minimum
- Weekly total: 7,000+ impressions
- Monthly follower growth needed: 770 to hit 7k by Dec 2026
- Comment velocity target: 5+ comments per post in first hour
